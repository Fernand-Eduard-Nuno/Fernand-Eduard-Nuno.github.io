About

Prof. Fernando Eduardo Nuno is a distinguished professor with a rich academic and professional background. With years of experience in teaching business management, he has contributed significantly to shaping the careers of many students and professionals.

His expertise lies in strategic management, leadership, and organizational behavior. Having worked with several educational institutions and corporate entities, Prof. Nuno bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

His research interests include business innovation, operational efficiency, and sustainable management practices.
